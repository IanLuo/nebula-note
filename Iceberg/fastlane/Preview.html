Description
We design x3 Note to help students, bloggers, writers, and professionals to capture ideas, manage plans, and transform throughts into organized articles.

It won't write story or article for you, but x3 Note will gather your ideas from any source from your phone, mac or iPad, so everytime you open x3 Note, your ideas will be there, waiting to inspire you.

With the ability to generate work plan from the note content, it feels like there's a clever and busy house keeper, tracking all your work status and time, and show them to you in one place with well orgnized and easy to read format, tells you what is more urgant, and when you decide to start one, directly take you to the exact location, like you never left.

Our text editor is very clean and also very smart.
- Patagraphs can fold, so you can keep it clean, even when the document is very long, and focus on the current work.
- Text decorations, like Bold, underscore, italic, strike though, hilightly.
- Plenty of attachments, image, video, audio, loction, sketch.
- Paragraph can have sub paragraphs, help you to organize the contents.
- And plenty of handy tools, such as list, checkbox, quote, source code, outline ext.

Captured ideas can be directly moved into the current editing location, all those ideas only one tap away, when you are working with one note, you can keep capturing new ideas, they all put in the same place, orgnized.
- Share any kind of resource from any app, like images, links, texts, videos, audios, you can use share function from other app, or just take a sccreenshot and send to x3 Note.
- All captured ideas can directly insert into your document.

When you are writing something, you don't have to face the situation of finish now, or never. instead, you can mark the paragraph as 'TODO', and safe to close the app, then x3 Note will find this paragraph, and show it on the agenda, with the title of the paragraph, so you know where to continue, by tapping on it, it will open your document, and take you to where you left your work.
- Add customizable status to paragraph
- Add date and time to paragraph, which will calculate and show you which one is more urgant
- Add priority to paragraph, so you manage your order by yourself
- All unfinished paragraphs will be gathered and displayed in agenda, with a order taken everythin into account.

- Orgnize paragraphs by type of status, wheather they are upcoming, overdue, or anything in between.
- Move document around, works like folders.
- Move paragraphs around, inside, or between documents.
- If those don't work, juse search it.
