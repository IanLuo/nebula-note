Description
Keeping excellent notes, writing great articles, the most important tool must be a handy, powerful editor. Other than that, for long term note keeping, or article writing, for most of them, it is a on going process, you need to keep collecting materials, making planning for you work, and keep writing, keep everything organized. These works are trivial, distractive, so it is important to form a set of working habbits and familiar tools, and to manage all thses planning, materials organizing and writings work well, with all the hard works, you can get greate notes or articles as a results and rewards.

With that in mind, Icetea note is desinged to help you, with the 3 major parts: Editor, Planning, and Idea capture. Those parts works together seamlessly, to provide ability to handle planning, materials organizing in one place, let you focusing on your editor, make planning and managing materials without switching working context, and pick up where you left from the agenda which automatically generated from your work contents.

- Editor
Lightweight markup launguage based rich text editor, Markdown like syntax, supports lots of attachment types, including image, sketch, audio, video, location, etc.
Supports text decorations: Bold, italic, underscore, strike through, highlight.
Support insert elements: link, checkbox, ordered list, unordered list, date and time, quote block, code block.
Folding and unfolding for sections.
Move sections freely in or between documents.
Outline.

- Capture ideas
Capture materials from any app that can share image, text, link, audio, video, or capture your idea with these forms by your self.

- Planning
Automatically create tasks based on contents that you put in your document, goto them directly and start work right away.
Content marks: section status, priority, tags, automatically categorize by status, tags, schedule date, due date, etc.

- Document management
Any depth of level of sub documents, organize them and move around freely. Note only the documents, but also the contents, can be moved around documents easily.

- iCloud sync
Automatically sync to your iCloud account, keep your data safe, and keep all devices the same.

- Export to other forms
HTML, txt, image, PDF, Markdown.

- And more to come.
